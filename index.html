    Save Canada Before It’s Too Late!

    Welcome to Annexation Hangman, the satirical word-guessing game where the fate of Canada hangs in the balance!

    The Premise

    In a world where President Coddled Chump and his crony billionaire Felon Husk are plotting to absorb Canada into the United States, you are the last hope to stop the annexation.

    Each incorrect guess brings Canada one step closer to becoming the 51st state. Provinces will flip one by one, with British Columbia always falling last. If you fail to guess the word in time, Canada is lost.

    How to Play

    • Guess letters to uncover the hidden word.
    • Each wrong guess brings Canada one step closer to annexation.
    • Provinces will turn blue as they fall under US control.
    • Guess the full word before all provinces are gone to save Canada!

    Will You Be Canada’s Hero?

    The choice is yours: Save Canada or watch it disappear under the stars and stripes.

    Good luck, and remember—this is not a drill!
